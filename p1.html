Artificial intelligence is a study of intelligent behavior in humans, animals, and machines.  It is used to find ways in which such behavior can be introduced to industrial machines.  Artificial intelligence is one of the most difficult and the most controversial topics of mankind.  It is difficult for artificial intelligence to research it may be more difficult than research to explore outer space.Many benefits are gained from this difficulty, whether on the intellectual or scientific side.  There are many applications on artificial intelligence, such as the launch of the space shuttle, as its launch depends on an artificial intelligence program.  Artificial intelligence has not achieved success in the world of advanced technology represented by space travel, but artificial intelligence systems have an important role in business and financial transactions.  It is also used in computer games and the film industryArtificial intelligence has many advantages and disadvantages. Among the advantages of artificial intelligence is that it enhances skills and provide the best service to customers, and many new innovations and inventions that affect daily life occur through artificial intelligence, and there are many industries that have sparked controversy through it, such as cars and airplanes. Among the disadvantages of artificial intelligence is the loss of jobs in terms of using artificial intelligence, the company does not need employees as it does the same tasks, increasing the difference in income between the poor and the rich, and it is used in the work of weapons that cause destruction.
